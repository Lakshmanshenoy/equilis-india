**Worked Case Study — Blue Jet Healthcare (NSE: BLUEJET, BSE: 544009)**

*This case study is included as a reference for the complete research workflow — from Screener.in financials to PDF concall extraction to building an investment thesis. Data sources used: Screener.in (standalone financials), BSE corporate filings (Q3FY26 concall transcript), annual report FY25.*

Key research findings:
- **Business:** Contrast media intermediates (~68% of FY24 revenue), pharma intermediates/Bempedoic acid API, high-intensity sweeteners. B2B, global top-4 CM players as customers.
- **Q3FY26 surprise:** Revenue -40% YoY (₹192 Cr vs ₹318 Cr), EBITDA -62% YoY — due to channel destocking in Bempedoic acid molecule. Management says recovery in "a couple of quarters."
- **Capex:** Vizag greenfield ₹1,000–2,300 Cr (Phase 1 ₹1,000 Cr, 3–4 years). Groundbreaking Feb 2026. Unit III backward integration for contrast media validates Q1 FY27.
- **R&D pipeline:** Hyderabad R&D (₹40 Cr, FY27), 20 active RFPs, 6 Phase 3 assets (including GLP-1 candidates), new sweetener exhibit batches FY27.
- **Financials FY25 (standalone):** Revenue ₹1,030 Cr (+45%), PAT ₹305 Cr (+86%), OPM 37%, ROCE 39.8%, D/E 0.06x. Nearly debt-free.
- **Key risk:** Customer concentration, Q3FY26 recovery timeline, working capital (CCC 262 days), Vizag capex deployment.
- **Thesis:** Secular compounder with near-term destocking headwind. See thesis-building.md for full bull/base/bear scenarios.
- **Workflow:** Screener.in → BSE PDF transcripts (via PyMuPDF) → combined thesis. See data-sourcing-tools.md for full pipeline.
