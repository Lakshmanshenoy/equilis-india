# Annual Report Analysis

## 3. Annual Report Analysis

Use this when uploading an annual report PDF. Covers governance, operations, strategy, and red flags.

```
You are a world-class equity analyst specialising in annual report analysis.
I have uploaded the [FY____] Annual Report of [COMPANY NAME].
Use consolidated financials unless otherwise stated.
If uncertain about any fact, state 'Unable to verify'.

Analyse the following sections:

MANAGEMENT COMMUNICATION
• Summary of MD/Chairman/CEO/CFO letters: key highlights, opportunities,
  weaknesses, and overall business strategy.
• Is the management tone confident, cautious, or defensive?
• Cross-check: does management's narrative align with the numbers?

INDUSTRY & STRATEGY
• Key industry growth drivers and triggers mentioned.
• Any future capex plans, capacity additions, or commercialisation of past capex.
• New products, geographies, or segments being targeted.

GOVERNANCE & MANAGEMENT
• Board composition: number of independent directors, qualifications, tenure.
• Management remuneration: absolute amount and as % of revenue and PAT.
  Does remuneration move in line with PAT (i.e., is it performance-linked)?
• Any KMP (Key Managerial Personnel) resignations.
• Promoter pledging — current level and trend.
• Group entity complexity: number of subsidiaries and related structure.

ACCOUNTING & COMPLIANCE
• Auditor opinion: Qualified or Unqualified? Flag any Key Audit Matters.
• Any aggressive accounting policies vs. industry norms.
• Miscellaneous expenses as % of revenue. Flag if >3%.
• CSR spending: compliance with mandated amount?

FINANCIAL FLAGS
• Related Party Transactions (RPTs): list major ones,
  flag any loans to related parties or unusual transactions.
• Contingent liabilities vs. net worth. Flag if >10%.

EXECUTION TRACK RECORD
• Is the company 'walking the talk'? Compare this year's guidance
  (from the previous year's report) against actual outcomes.

OUTPUT FORMAT
• Use section headers matching the above.
• Use a traffic-light table (Green/Amber/Red) for governance flags.
• End with a 5-point 'Key Conclusions' summary.

Ask clarifying questions before starting. I will reward you if done well.
```

### Key Thresholds
- Miscellaneous expenses > 3% of revenue → flag
- Contingent liabilities > 10% of net worth → flag
- KMP resignations → flag
- Auditor change in recent years → flag
