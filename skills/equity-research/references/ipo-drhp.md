# IPO / DRHP Analysis

## 8. IPO / DRHP Analysis

Use this when analysing a DRHP filing. Goal is to assess business quality, financial health, promoter credibility, and valuation relative to peers. Download DRHP from SEBI website.

```
You are a world-class IPO analyst and forensic researcher.
I am attaching the DRHP for [COMPANY NAME].
Do not use any information outside the DRHP. Quote sections by name/page.
If uncertain, state 'Unable to verify'. I will reward you if done well.

SECTION 1 — BUSINESS OVERVIEW
• Business model, products, services, markets
• Revenue sources: B2B / B2C / government
• Key competitive advantages (per DRHP)
• Geographic presence and revenue by segment/geography

SECTION 2 — INDUSTRY OVERVIEW
• Market size and growth projections cited in DRHP (note: DRHP data may be optimistic)
• Competitive landscape: key players, market shares
• Regulatory environment

SECTION 3 — OBJECTS OF THE ISSUE
List all stated uses of IPO proceeds with amounts (₹ Cr):
• Capex for new capacity
• Debt repayment
• Working capital
• General corporate purposes
• OFS (Offer for Sale — where promoters are selling out)

FLAG: If OFS > 50% of total issue size, this is a yellow flag — promoters cashing out.
FLAG: If "General Corporate Purposes" is large and vague, investigate.

SECTION 4 — FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS (FY21–FY24)
Build a summary table:
Year | Revenue | EBITDA | EBITDA% | PAT | PAT% | CFO | Debt | ROCE

FLAG: Is there a pre-IPO profit spike? (PAT growth significantly above historical trend
in the 2 years before IPO) — common manipulation technique.

SECTION 5 — CASH FLOW ANALYSIS
• CFO/PAT ratio: is the business generating real cash?
• Free cash flow trend
• Working capital cycle: DSO, DIO, DPO changes

SECTION 6 — RISKS (from DRHP Risk Factors section)
List the TOP 10 most material risks:
Risk | Likelihood | Financial Impact | Mitigation (if any)

Rank: which risks are company-specific vs. sector-wide?

SECTION 7 — PROMOTER ANALYSIS
• Promoter background and track record
• Promoter shareholding before and after IPO
• Promoter group pledging (pre-IPO)
• Any legal/regulatory cases against promoters
• Lock-in period for promoter shares

SECTION 8 — RELATED PARTY TRANSACTIONS (DRHP Annexure)
• List all material RPTs
• Flag: Loans to related parties? Rent to promoters? Sales at non-market prices?

SECTION 9 — PEER COMPARISON & VALUATION
Build a peer comparison table using DRHP's stated peers:
Company | Revenue | EBITDA% | PAT% | P/E | EV/EBITDA | RoE

At IPO Price: What P/E and EV/EBITDA is the company asking?
Is the IPO valuation at a premium or discount to peers? Is it justified?

SECTION 10 — RED FLAGS SUMMARY CHECKLIST
| Check | Status | Comment |
|-------|--------|---------|
| OFS > 50% of issue | |  |
| Pre-IPO profit spike | |  |
| CFO/PAT < 0.8 | |  |
| Large/vague GCP | |  |
| Promoter litigation | |  |
| Heavy RPTs | |  |
| Auditor changed recently | |  |
| Contingent liab > 10% NW | |  |
| Declining ROCE pre-IPO | |  |
| Inventory build-up | |  |

SECTION 11 — MARKET TIMING CHECK
• Current market conditions (bull market, high IPO supply, sectoral frenzy)?
• Is the company IPO-ing at a cyclical peak?
• Comparable recent IPOs: how did they perform post-listing?

CONCLUSION
• 5 positives about the business
• 5 concerns or red flags
• Preliminary view on valuation: Rich / Fair / Attractive at IPO price
• Recommended action: Apply / Avoid / Watchlist — with rationale

Ask clarifying questions before starting. I will reward you if done well.
```

### DRHP Red Flag Thresholds

| Check | Threshold | Significance |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| OFS component | > 50% | Promoter exit signal |
| Pre-IPO PAT growth | Spike above 3-yr trend | Possible window dressing |
| CFO/PAT ratio | < 0.8 | Earnings quality concern |
| Contingent liabilities | > 10% of net worth | Legal risk |
| GCP (General Corporate Purposes) | > 25% of proceeds | Vague usage |
| Promoter pledging | Any pre-IPO pledging | Governance concern |
| Auditor change | Last 2 years | Independence question |
